The Puppy Bowl Adds Keyboard Cat Halftime Show

Publish date: 2024-09-07
PUPPY BOWL/KITTY HALF-TIME 9

While the original Keyboard Cat has long since retired his T-shirt, another cat will take up his mantle and perform during Animal Planet’s Puppy Bowl X kitten halftime show February 2, leaving us to theorize that “Keyboard Cat” is a code name that can be passed on from kitty to kitty. He’s like James Bond, but with a tiny hat. The piano-playing feline will be joined by Lil Bub and a cadre of penguin cheerleaders, because Animal Planet has its finger on America’s pulse and is going HAM against the Kitten Bowl.
