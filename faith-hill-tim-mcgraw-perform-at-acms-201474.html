Tim McGraw, Faith Hill Duet at ACM Awards: &quot;Meanwhile Back at Mama's&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-29

More Soul2Soul than ever! Tim McGraw and Faith Hill once again squashed any false reports of marital trouble — this time in song — at the ACM Awards in Las Vegas on Sunday April 6. Married for 17 years with three daughters, the spouses crooned McGraw’s new single, “Meanwhile Back at Mama’s” in a surprise duet.

Clad in a tux and black cowboy hat, McGraw, 46, began the performance solo before his wife, also 46, joined him onstage in a white sequined gown. They topped off the performance with an onstage smooch.
