CaCee Cobb Donald Fasion Wedding Video: Jessica Simpson's BFF Is Married

Publish date: 2024-08-16

It’s been five months since CaCee Cobb and Donald Faison tied the knot in close pal Zach Braff‘s backyard, and it’s been a whirlwind ever since! Jessica Simpson‘s former personal assistant and The Exes actor, who are currently expecting their first child together, exchanged vows on Dec. 15, 2012 in L.A. The couple’s big day is featured in the summer issue of Martha Stewart Weddings, and to celebrate, videographer Wade of Shark Pig has released a sneak peek from their ceremony and reception.

The video shows glimpses of Cacee and Jessica (who is pregnant with her second child) primping before the ceremony. It also shows Faison and his groomsmen horsing around on Braff’s property.

Cobb, 35, and Faison, 38, recited personal vows during the Southern-style affair, attended by celebrity pals Ashlee Simpson, Seth Green, Stacey Dash, Breckin Meyer, Elisa Donovan and Sarah Chalke. Singer-songwriter Joshua Radin serenaded the newlyweds during their first dance.
