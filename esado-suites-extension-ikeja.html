Esado Suites Extension, Ikeja

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Esado Suites Extension is close to several shopping and business districts in Ikeja. It offers classy and comfortable lodgings at affordable rates. It can be found at 2, Akingbola Street, Off Olanrewaju, Oregun, Lagos. Guests can have a beautiful movie experience at Silverbird Cinemas, and treat themselves to tasty snacks at Domino’s Pizza, which is near Esado Suites Extension.

The accommodations have been furnished to give a simple yet homely feel. The room categories offered by Esado Suites Extension include Standard Room and Executive Room. Guests would find an air conditioner, a study desk, a king-sized bed, a wardrobe, a flat screen TV with satellite reception and a bathroom with a shower.

Esado Suites Extension serves continental breakfast to guests at a surcharge. Guests can order for the meal of their choice at the restaurant. Esado Suites Extension has a bar where guests can order a drink or two while unwinding at the lounge. A business centre is available where guests can get printing and photocopying services. Free Wi-Fi access has been provided in the rooms and in selected public areas.

Onsite parking is available. Guests can request for concierge, luggage storage, laundry/dry cleaning, airport pickup and 24 hours front desk services. Esado Suites Extension provides round-the-clock electricity and security.
