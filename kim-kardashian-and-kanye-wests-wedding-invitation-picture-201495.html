Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's Wedding Invitation: Picture

Publish date: 2024-08-10

It’s Kimye’s version of a Golden Ticket! As one of the most famous couples in the world, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West know literally thousands of people who would love to attend their much-anticipated Paris wedding on May 24. Of those thousands, approximately 100 were given spots on the guest list. Invitations were dispatched to the elite group in mid-April — and Us Weekly has an exclusive look at the coveted memento.

The invitations feature a simple design: gold lettering on a neutral background. Information is scarce; all that’s included are a few lines about the date and dress code for the ceremony and rehearsal dinner.

As Us Weekly previously reported in April, the happy couple are keeping most details under wraps until their friends and family arrive in France. “Guests won’t know the venue until they get to Paris,” a source shared last month. “[Kim] hasn’t even told her sisters.”

Engaged since Kardashian’s 33rd birthday in October, the reality star and her fiance, 36, have been preparing for their nuptials for months.
