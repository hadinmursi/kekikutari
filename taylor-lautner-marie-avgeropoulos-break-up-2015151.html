Taylor Lautner, Marie Avgeropoulos Break Up

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Taylor Lautner‘s back on the market! The actor and his costar girlfriend Marie Avgeropoulos have broken up after over a year of dating, Us Weekly has confirmed.

The couple started dating in the summer of 2013 after playing love interests in the movie Tracers, due to be released in March of this year. The pair, who never spoke publicly about their romance, but who were photographed together on numerous occasions while they were dating, split a few months ago, as E! first reported.

A source tells Us that the split was entirely amicable. The hunky actor is currently dating, but is not in any exclusive relationship at the moment.
