ISPW Wrestling Coming To Totowa, NJ On 11/19; Rich Swann To Compete

Publish date: 2024-09-03

ISPW Wrestling will be live at The Totowa PAL in Totowa, NJ on November 19.

Stars like Rich Swann, Crowbar, and second-generation star Miranda Gordy will be in action. ROH wrestlers LSG and Cheeseburger (World Famous CB) will also compete. Plus, former WWE Superstar Maven will return to the ring at the show.

ISPW passed along the following:

ISPW Wrestling will be LIVE at The Totowa PAL on Friday Night, November 19th! The Main Event will be The Championship Rumble ( 20-Man Royal Rumble Style Match) to crown an ISPW World Heavyweight Champion! WWE Hall of Famer Tito Santana will be appearing live to lay down the law and order as ISPW Commissioner! Rich Swann collides with Crowbar for the first time ever!

Terry Gordy’s daughter Miranda Gordy makes her New Jersey debut challenging Vicious Vicki for the ISPW Women’s Title! “Dangerous” Danny Davis & Maven will also return to the ring for The Championship Rumble! Independent Wrestling Lifetime Achivement Awards! ECW Originals Collide when Danny Doring takes on HC Loc! Former WWE NXT Star Bull James! A Ladder Match to crown a ISPW Tri-State Champion! All this and SO MUCH MORE!
