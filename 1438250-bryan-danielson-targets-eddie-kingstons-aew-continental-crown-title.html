Bryan Danielson Targets Eddie Kingstons AEW Continental Crown Title

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Bryan Danielson is not finished with Eddie Kingston.

The feud between Bryan Danielson and Eddie Kingston has heated up in recent weeks. Kingston beat Danielson to advance in the AEW Continental Classic. Danielson and Claudio Castagnoli got revenge, as they defeated Kingston and Ortiz on the January 20 episode of AEW Collision.

In a tweet, AEW shared a video in which Bryan Danielson was asked why he spit in Eddie Kingston’s face after the match. Castagnoli was standing by Danielson, and he noted that the real question was why hadn’t he done it sooner. Danielson replied and emphasized that he did not respect Kingston.

“Yeah, I suppose that is the real question,” Bryan Danielson said. “Here’s the thing. Eddie might have won the Continental Classic, Eddie might have beat me once. He’s lucky. But that doesn’t mean that I respect Eddie Kingston. I do not respect Eddie Kingston. You asked me why I spit in his face? It’s because I do not respect Eddie Kingston. He knows that. He knows that him beating me was a fluke, and he knows that I’m coming for those titles.”

Kingston won the AEW Continental Classic by defeating Jon Moxley at AEW Worlds End. In doing so, he won the AEW Continental Crown Championship.
