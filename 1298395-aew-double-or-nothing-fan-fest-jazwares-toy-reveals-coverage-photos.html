AEW Double Or Nothing Fan Fest Jazwares Toy Reveals &amp; Coverage (Photos)

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Today was AEW’s Double or Nothing Fan Fest and Jazwares brought the fire with new action figure reveals!

Jazwares AEW Reveals

Jazwares, whom makes the AEW action figures/toys if you didn’t know, typically reveals a lot of new action figures and products at the Fan Fest events. Today they revealed a ton of upcoming lineups as well as showed new prototype images and digital renders. The physical figures seen below are hand painted as they are in early production, so the final details of the figures will look even better.
