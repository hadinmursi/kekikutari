Tallulah Willis Has a Tattoo of Tony Hawks Name on Her Butt

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Cheeky? Tallulah Willis appears to be a big Tony Hawk fan. Demi Moore’s youngest daughter, 21, got the pro skater’s name tattooed on her booty.

In a revealing, butt-baring Instagram photo posted on Thursday, Dec. 10, Willis showed off her new ink while flipping off the camera. “@TONYHAWK,” she captioned the photo.

Bruce Willis’ daughter also got a tattoo on her elbow that reads, “obviously magic.” “Obvi @seanfromtexas,” she captioned the Instagram, giving West Hollywood, Calif., based tattoo artist Sean at Tattoo Mania a shout-out.
