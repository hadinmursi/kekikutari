Gigi Hadid Suffers Wardrobe Malfunction, Nip Slip on Versace Runway

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Oops! Gigi Hadid suffered a wardrobe malfunction while walking in the Versace show during Milan Fashion Week on Friday, February 26. The chain-link strap on her dress slipped off the 20-year-old model’s right shoulder and exposed her bare breast.

Despite the nip slip snafu, the Sports Illustrated stunner continued strutting her stuff and ended the show by giving designer Donatella Versace a high-five.

“Wadrobe malfunctions happen on the runway every day of fw & are…unfortunate,lol,but let’s talk instead about the new VERSACE CHOKERS😍😜✌,” Hadid tweeted after the show. “& the bags & the boots & the coats & everythinnngggg, killin me Donatella @Versace !!!!!!! 😍😍”

Hadid’s model pals Kendall Jenner and Karlie Kloss also walked in the show, but avoided wardrobe mishaps!
