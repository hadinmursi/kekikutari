Meet Lily Aldridge, Caleb Followill's Daughter Dixie Pearl!

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Lily Aldridge teased her Twitter fans with a picture of her baby girl's feet in July, but on August 18, the Victoria's Secret supermodel finally unveiled the first photo of Dixie Pearl via the social networking site.

"Glad everyone liked our family pic," she wrote in a follow-up tweet. "She's our angel!"

Born in Nashville June 21, Dixie Pearl is the first child for Aldridge, 26, and Kings of Leon frontman Caleb Followill, 30, who wed at San Ysidro Ranch in Montecito, Calif. in May 2011. "They are so happy," a source recently Us Weekly of the new parents. "The baby is adorable and healthy. . . Caleb seems to really be enjoying fatherhood."
