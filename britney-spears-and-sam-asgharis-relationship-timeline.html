Britney Spears and Sam Asgharis Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-08-18

It was love at first sight when Britney Spears and Sam Asghari met in October 2016 on the set of her “Slumber Party” music video, but the fairy-tale does not have a happy ending.

Spears was charmed when chatting with Asghari and the pair exchanged phone numbers and continued to get to know each other. They made their relationship official in January 2017.

Throughout Spears’ ups and downs, Asghari acted as a huge support system for Spears in her personal and professional life. “Sam has been an absolute dream come true for Britney,” a source told Us Weekly exclusively in May 2019. “He is such a positive light in her life.”

Asghari proposed to Spears in September 2021 after nearly five years together. They later walked down the aisle in June 2022.
