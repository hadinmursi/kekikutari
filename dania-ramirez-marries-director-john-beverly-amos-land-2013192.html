Dania Ramirez Marries Director John Beverly Amos Land!

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Dania Ramirez is officially off the market. The 33-year-old Heroes actress wed 35-year-old director/writer John Beverly Amos Land (known as Bev) in the Dominican Republic on Saturday, Feb. 16, Ramirez's rep confirms to Us Weekly.

"It was magical," Ramirez tweeted of the Colin Cowie-designed ceremony, which took place on a beach at the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Punta Cana. The actress announced their engagement in January 2012. "The love of my life found me!" Ramirez told Latina after Land's proposal. "That's how he would say it anyway since he claims he was attracted to my energy before I even turned around."

It was love at first sight for Ramirez. "The moment I turned around, he casually asked me and our mutual friend to join him for lunch and once we started talking I knew I could talk to him forever and we haven't stopped talking since!" she recalled. "And, the fact that he's ubersmart and has the most amazing son in the world, there was no doubt I was totally in.”

Land is the grandson of Aflac founder John Beverly Amos. Ramirez told Latina she feels as though she and the filmmaker have been together in many other lifetimes. "I'm just glad we finally found each other in this one," the American Reunion actress gushed.
