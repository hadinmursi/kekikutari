Road Warrior Animal Comments On The Ascension, Finn Balor Bringing Back Body Paint?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Road Warrior Animal Comments On The Ascension

Road Warrior Animal, one half of the Legion of Doom, recently spoke with ABC2 News out of Baltimore to promote his upcoming appearance with Maryland Championship Wrestling this weekend.

Animal spoke about being a part of the Road Warriors, coming to MCW and other topics, including his thoughts on new WWE tag team The Ascension. Regarding the duo, Animal said: 

“I wish The Ascension nothing but the best and hope they are successful. I know they are just doing what they are told, but when they run down teams like the Road Warriors and Demolition before ever accomplishing anything, they will be dead in the water. The fans today are just too smart for that and you can tell by their reaction. You have to look the part. Hawk and I did and we were allowed to be an extension of ourselves. The Ascension is being fed a part by Hollywood writers, not all of whom understand the business. At the same time, imitation is the sincerest form of flattery.”

Finn Balor Update

It is expected than Finn Balor will bring back body paint and a more theatrical entrance when he faces Adrian Neville at NXT Takeover: Rival next week. 

Balor is known for using body paint on the independent scene, a look he carried over to NXT and used at the NXT December live special. Balor was said to be reserving the grander entrance and paint for bigger NXT shows; he used a “Carnage” themed look (Marvel) at the December show but there is no word of what he may use this time around.
