Gigi Hadid Denies She Was Snorting Cocaine in Cody Simpson's Snapchat

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Oh snap! Gigi Hadid took to Twitter to bash the blog that claimed she was caught snorting cocaine in her boyfriend Cody Simpson’s Snapchat video over the weekend.

“I take pride in being a good role model, would never touch that, & will not stand for or put up with a little piece of scum blog like OU,” the 19-year-old model tweeted. Hadid’s denial was in reference to OceanUp’s post which shows the 10-second video of Simpson’s, in which Yolanda Foster’s daughter bends her face down to the DJ’s table at a Victoria’s Secret Pink spring break party in Miami.

“If u actually watch the video there is nothing on the counter .5 sec’s before you see that image…It’s not only impossible but insane to me,” she explained to fans after the questionable video surfaced. “Besides for doing the supposed in general, I’d be an idiot to do that at a WORK EVENT in front of hundreds of people/press. I’m insulted.”
