What is the age difference between Hoda and Jenna?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Hoda and Jenna Bush Hager are almost 20 years apart in terms of age difference. Jenna Welch Bush Hager is 41 years old as of 2023 and was born on November 25, 1981, in Dallas, Texas, United States.

Hoda Kotb is 58 years old as of 2023. Hoda Kotb was born on August 9, 1964, in Norman, Oklahoma, United States of America.

Jenna Welch Bush Hager is an American news personality, author, and journalist. She is the co-host of Today with Hoda & Jenna, the fourth hour of NBC’s morning news program Today. Hager

Hoda Kotb is an Egyptian-American broadcast journalist, television personality, and author best known as the main co-anchor of the NBC News morning show Today.

In April 1998, Hoda Kotb officially joined NBC News as a correspondent for Dateline NBC and all NBC News platforms.

Hoda Kotb started her job as the host of a live, one-hour show twice a week on Sirius XM’s Today channel on February 23, 2015.

On January 2, 2018, Hoda Kotb was named co-anchor of Today with Savannah Guthrie. They make up the first female anchor duo to lead the show.
