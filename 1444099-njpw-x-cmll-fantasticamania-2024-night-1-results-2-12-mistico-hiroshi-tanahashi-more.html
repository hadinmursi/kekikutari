NJPW x CMLL Fantasticamania 2024 Night 1 Results (2/12): Mistico, Hiroshi Tanahashi, More

Publish date: 2024-08-15

On February 12, NJPW and CMLL kicked off the Fantasticamania tour with the first night of action from Osaka Prefectural Gym #2 (EDION Arena Osaka) in Osaka, Japan.

The results, courtesy of Cagematch, are as follows:

  • Difunto & DOUKI defeat BULLET CLUB (BONE SOLDIER Jr. & Gedo)
  • : Magnus vs, Tiger Mask ended in a time-limit draw.
  • Los Ingobernables de Japon (BUSHI, Tetsuya Naito & Titan) & KAMAITACHI defeat Brillante Jr., El Desperado, Ryusuke Taguchi & Stigma 
  • OKUMURA, Strong Machine J & YOH defeat Dark Panther, Pegasso & Tomoaki Honma
  • Hechicero, Rocky Romero & Soberano Jr. defeat Atlantis Jr., Templario & Volador Jr.
  • Hiroshi Tanahashi, Mascara Dorada & Mistico defeat Francesco Akira, Stuka Jr. & Ultimo Guerrero
