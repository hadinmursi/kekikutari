Former Real Housewives of D.C. Stars: Where Are They Now?

Publish date: 2024-08-13

It’s been over a decade since Bravo fans met and subsequently said goodbye to The Real Housewives of D.C.

The reality series, which starred Mary Amons, Lynda Erkiletian, Cat Ommanney, Michaele Salahi and Stacie Scott Turner, premiered in August 2010. The show ran for 11 episodes and wrapped up with a two-part reunion in October 2010.

While the franchise was short-lived, it was full of drama — on and offscreen. In November 2009, Michaele and then-husband Tareq Salahi made headlines for crashing Barack Obama’s first State Dinner. Andy Cohen has been open about the fact that the situation overshadowed the show, prompting its early cancellation.
