Karen Khachanov Wikipedia Explored

Karen Khachanov is a professional tennis player from Russia. After reaching the semifinals of the 2019 French Open, Khachanov attained his career-high singles ranking of world No. 8 on July 15, 2019.

Khachanov is a hard-hitting baseliner who is recognized for his serve and groundstrokes. He prefers to be attacking, setting up victories with his serve, or dictating rallies with his forehand.

Khachanov was trained by Igor Bitsenko in Moscow and Vedran Marti in Split, Croatia, for some time. In 2014, he joined Galo Blanco’s 4Slam Tennis Academy. In November 2017, Khachanov and Blanco parted ways. He worked out with his old instructor, Vedran Marti, for a few years. José Clavet is his current coach.
