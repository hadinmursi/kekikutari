Jaden Smith Describes Relationship With Kylie Jenner: &quot;It's Pretty Awesome!&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Jaden Smith is opening up about his fledgling relationship with Kylie Jenner for the very first time in the May issue of Wonderland magazine. In early March, Us Weekly exclusively reported that the 14-year-old After Life actor (son of Will Smith and Jada Pinkett-Smith) and the 15-year-old Keeping Up With the Kardashians star (daughter of Bruce and Kris Jenner) were dating.

"I was in L.A., we were all hanging out. Then I went to New York and she came too, then she came here [to London] and surprised me, so that's pretty baller," Jaden said of Kylie. "She's one of my best friends. It's pretty awesome."
