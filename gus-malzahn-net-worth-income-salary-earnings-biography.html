Arthur Gustav “Gus” Malzahn, III (born October 28, 1965) is an American football coach and former player. Malzahn is presently the head coach at Auburn University, a job he accepted prior to the 2013 season. He spent the 2012 season as the head football coach at Arkansas State University. From 2009 through 2011, Malzahn served as the offensive coordinator at Auburn University. In 2010, a season in which the Auburn Tigers won the national title, Malzahn got the Broyles Award, which honours the outstanding assistant coach in college football. Prior to his tenure at Arkansas State and Auburn, Malzahn worked as offensive coordinator at the University of Arkansas and the University of Tulsa
