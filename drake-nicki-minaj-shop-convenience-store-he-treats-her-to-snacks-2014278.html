Drake, Nicki Minaj Shop Convenience Store, He Treats Her to Snacks

Publish date: 2024-09-06

She can have whatever she likes! Nicki Minaj recently had an epic trip to a local convenience store, thanks to the world’s best shopping buddy, Drake. 

The two rappers were in a corner store while Minaj, 31, was filming “She Came to Give It to You,” her new music video with Usher, off the R&B crooner’s upcoming album. 

Taking a break from the production, Drizzy, 27, treated Minaj to a stroll through the aisles of chips and candy. “Get whatever you want,” Drake said, spoiling the “Anaconda” performer, who gave him a sexy lap dance in the recently unveiled video for the song. 

The behind-the-scenes clip continues as Minaj, wearing a sexy, sheer, body-hugging black dress, grabs a bag of Cheez-Its, before Drake continues his generosity, telling her, “All you gotta do is point!” and asking if Minaj’s friend wants anything as well.
