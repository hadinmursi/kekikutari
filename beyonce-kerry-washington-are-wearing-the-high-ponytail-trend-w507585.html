Beyonce, Kerry Washington Are Wearing the High Ponytail Trend

Publish date: 2024-09-06

The I Dream of Jeannie high ponytail is making a comeback! Some are going for texture, others are opting for half-up styles, and a few are even adding accessories! Copy any or all — no matter which way you go, you’re sure to look cute, youthful and entirely on-trend.
