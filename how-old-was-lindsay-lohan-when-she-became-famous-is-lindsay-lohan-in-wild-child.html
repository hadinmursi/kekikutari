How old was Lindsay Lohan when she became famous? Is Lindsay Lohan in Wild Child?

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Lindsay Lohan was only 12 years old when her big breakout role in Disney’s The Parent Trap (1998) premiered in movie theaters. In 2004, when Lohan was 17, she became the youngest host of the MTV Movie Awards.

Lindsay Lohan was not cast in the Wild Child film. As a child model, Lindsay Lohan appeared in over 60 television commercials for brands like Pizza Hut and Wendy’s, as well as a Jell-O spot with Bill Cosby.

Lindsay Lohan made appearances in the television films Life-Size (2000) and Get a Clue (2002), and the big-screen productions Freaky Friday (2003) and Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen (2004).

Lindsay Lohan’s 2004 debut album, Speak sold 1 million units in the United States where it peaked at number four on the Billboard 200, selling 261,762 copies in its first week.

The teen comedy sleeper hit Mean Girls (2004) affirmed Lindsay Lohan’s status as a teen idol in Hollywood. Lindsay Lohan starred in the comedies Herbie: Fully Loaded (2005) and Just My Luck (2006).
