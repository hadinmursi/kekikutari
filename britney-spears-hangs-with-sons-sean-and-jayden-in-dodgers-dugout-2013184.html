Britney Spears Hangs With Sons Sean and Jayden in Dodgers Dugout

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Britney Spears is a supermom! The 31-year-old pop star took her two sons, 7-year-old Sean and 6-year-old Jayden, to meet the Los Angeles Dodgers when the baseball team played the San Diego Padres at Dodger Stadium Apr. 17.

Spears tweeted a picture of her family hanging out in the dugout, where they ate cotton candy and chewed Quench Gum. The photo features the singer’s sons wearing matching jerseys over their flannel shirts, and the trio each wore hats bearing the team’s logo.

The twice-married Grammy winner also posed for a picture with outfielder Andre Ethier and closer Brandon League. (Spears’ boyfriend, David Lucado, was not present at the athletic event.)

Earlier that day, Spears confirmed she will be releasing a new song, “Ooh La La,” for The Smurfs 2.  “I have always loved the Smurfs as a kid and now my boys are the biggest Smurfs fans ever,” she told Us Weekly in a statement. “I wanted to surprise them with a song in the movie. I know they’ll think it’s Smurftastic!”

Smurfs 2 director Raja Gosnell added, “I am very excited that Britney is joining our smurfy adventure. ‘Ooh La La’ is the perfect Smurf party song, and the perfect button on the film for audiences around the world.”
