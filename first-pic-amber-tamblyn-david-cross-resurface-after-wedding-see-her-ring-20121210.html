FIRST PIC: Amber Tamblyn, David Cross Resurface After Wedding: See Her Ring!

Publish date: 2024-08-12

It's Mr. and Mrs. Cross!

On Friday, newlyweds Amber Tamblyn and David Cross took in the crisp autumn temperatures in Manhattan, taking a sweet walk together. In the first snaps of the couple since their Oct. 7 nuptials, the House, M.D. actress, 29, flashed her engagement and wedding band from the Arrested Development star, 48.

For the quirky couple's "I do's," Amblyn memorably donned a canary-yellow dress and went barefoot, arriving at the lakeside ceremony via canoe.
