Rapper OJ da Juiceman has been arrested and locked up on the charge of Gun and drug abuse.

OJ da Juiceman, originally named Otis Williams Jr, is a rapper.

Despite his real name, he is known for his stage name and his work with rapper Gucci Mane. Additionally, the rapper is also the founder of Vanity label 32 Entertainment.

However, fans are curious to know about the rapper, not because of his rap career. According to the outlet, he was arrested and locked up in the Hardin County Detention Centre. Let us learn more about it below!

Rapper OJ da Juiceman Arrested And Locked Up On Gun And Drug Abuse

Rapper OJ da Juiceman was arrested, according to the source.

The rapper ended up on the wrong side of the law. The local news stated that ‘he was charged with possession of a handgun by a convicted felon, possession of marijuana, and possession of drug paraphernalia.” Following that, he was detained in the Hardin County Detention Centre.

Further details surrounding his arrest are still unknown. However, this will undoubtedly impact the rapper’s recent video promotion. He shared a video promoting his Mother’s Day show on his Instagram story.

Meanwhile, OJ Da Juiceman is reportedly set to return to court on May 13.

Who Is OJ da Juiceman?

OJ da Juiceman, aged 40, is a rapper who recently got arrested and locked up for the guns and drug abuse.

He was birthed in 1981 in Georgia, US. He celebrates his birthday every year on November 23 with his family and friends. Talking about OJ’s family, he was raised by a single mother in East Atlanta, Atlanta.

Juiceman rose to fame in the early 2000s alongside his longtime friend Gucci Mane. The pair then repeatedly collaborated and partnered on singles together. These days, the Juiceman is operating his independent record label, 32 Entertainment.

However, before that, he began his music career with “Never Again Records,” widely known for their 2004 Summer spin-off hit “Black Tee.” Furthermore, he has released six mixtapes under 32 Entertainment and featured on many underground mixtapes featuring various artists.
