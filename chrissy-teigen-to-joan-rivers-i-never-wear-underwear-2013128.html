Chrissy Teigen Never Wears Underwear, Supermodel Tells Joan Rivers

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Chrissy Teigen never ever wears underwear. Not even when she's on a TV talk show wearing a mini dress. Not even during that time of the month.

In a super-revealing interview with Joan Rivers on E!'s Fashion Police on Friday, Aug. 9, the Sports Illustrated swimsuit model explained that she goes commando simply because she prefers not to wear panties. "I just don't, I don't like it," she succinctly told the stunned hosts, including Giuliana Rancic and George Kotsiopoulos, and fellow guest Ashley Benson.

When Rivers asked Teigen if she was currently wearing panties, the model, 27, replied in the negative. "I [just] got a nice a view," Kotsiopoulos quipped.
